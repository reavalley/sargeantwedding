Directions to the Church:

Postcode for Sat Navs – SY5 9HN You will depart the A5 at the first exit of the Woodcote Roundabout signposted for Montgomery on the B4386 follow the road for approximately 10 miles passing through the villages of Knox, Yockleton and Westbury until you reach the village of Worthen where you will find the church located on the left as you pass through coming from the direction of Shrewsbury.

Directions to the Reception

If you use google maps as your sat nav please the link provided and click get directions. Get Directions Postcode for the Reception: SY5 9JG The postcode provided is for the farm and not the field where the reception will be held so as you approach your destination look out for Signposts directing you to the reception. You will depart the A5 at the first exit of the Woodcote Roundabout signposted for Montgomery on the B4386 follow the road for approximately 10 miles passing through the villages of Knox, Yockleton, Westbury, Worthen and then Brockton after exiting Brockton carry on the B4386 for about a mile and you will see the Reception marquee on your left
